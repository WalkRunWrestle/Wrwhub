New here? How WRW works
Everything you need to know before you start your challenge.
1
Track your walk or run using Strava or any fitness app you like.
2
Take a screenshot or keep clear proof of each session.
3
Log each session inside your WRW challenge tracker.
4
When the full challenge is complete, send your proof.
You can split the run: complete your challenge over one or more sessions.
Proof is needed at the end: send your proof when the full challenge is complete.
Your medal follows completion: once your challenge is complete and proof is sent, we dispatch your medal.
Any tracker is fine: Strava is recommended, but any app or device that clearly shows your distance works.
Unlock collectible cards: every challenge includes milestone rewards. Track your progress and view your unlocked cards anytime in your Card Vault.
