Before you start
Track your walk or run in Strava or any fitness app, log each session inside your challenge, then send proof when you finish.
1
Track your session Use Strava or any fitness app or watch that clearly shows your distance.
2
Take a screenshot Save proof of each walk or run so you’ve got it ready when you need it.
3
Log your distance Open your WRW challenge and add each session until the full distance is complete.
4
Send proof and finish strong When your challenge is done, send your proof and we’ll take it from there.
Download Strava (Android) Download Strava (iPhone)

WRW is not affiliated with or endorsed by Strava. We recommend it for tracking, but you can use any app or device you wish that clearly shows your distance.

Challenge rules
A few things to know before you start logging sessions.
Distance can be split: complete your challenge over one or more sessions.
Proof is needed at the end: send proof when the full challenge is complete.
Your medal follows completion: medals are dispatched after challenge completion and proof submission.
Any tracker is fine: Strava is recommended, but any app or device that clearly shows distance works.
